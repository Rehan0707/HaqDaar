Statement

Context

Portable Welfare
Access System

Welfare schemes in India are largely tied to the state or district where you registered. When someone migrates for work, they often lose access to benefits they're entitled to. Not because they're ineligible, but because the systems don't talk to each other and nobody helped them navigate the transfer. Migrant workers are already the most vulnerable group. Losing welfare on top of relocation is genuinely harmful.

What Teams Should Build

A unified access layer that lets someone find and claim their benefits regardless of where they currently are. Portability without bureaucratic dead ends.
